For citation.is, the most directly useful resources and partner programs fall into four buckets: **data integrations**, **AI/agent infrastructure**, **open-science platforms**, and **developer ecosystems**.

### 1. Data integrations (for expanding beyond biology)
| Partner | Why it’s useful | What to integrate |
|---|---|---|
| **Crossref** | Nonprofit that assigns DOIs and makes scholarly content findable/citable. | DOI metadata, article-level citations.  [lib.dmu](https://lib.dmu.edu/su/opencitations) |
| **PubMed / NCBI** | Core biomedical literature and taxonomy. | Already used; expand to ClinicalTrials.gov, PMC full text.  [citation](https://citation.is) |
| **UniProt / PubChem** | Protein and compound reference data. | Already used; add ChEMBL, DrugBank for medicine.  [citation](https://citation.is) |
| **OpenAIRE** | Open-science citation metadata + research data/graph. | OA literature, grants, datasets, policy documents.  [lib.dmu](https://lib.dmu.edu/su/opencitations) |
| **Lens.org** | 200M+ scholarly records + 120M patents linked to citations. | Patents, scholarly works, regulatory documents.  [lib.dmu](https://lib.dmu.edu/su/opencitations) |
| **FRED / World Bank / IMF** | Economic indicators and macro data. | Add for economics verification engine. (general knowledge) |
| **CourtListener / Westlaw** | Case law and statutes. | Add for law verification engine. (general knowledge) |

### 2. AI/agent infrastructure (for MCP + distribution)

|---|---|---|

| **AIBase (citation repos)** | GitHub-style repo hub for AI citation tools. | List citation.is as an open citation project; connect to Gpt Researcher, RAG-API.  [aibase](https://www.aibase.com/repos/topic/citations) |

### 3. Open-science & citation platforms (for visibility + trust)
| Partner | Why it’s useful | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| **OpenCitations** | Open citation databases for scholarly references. | Submit your claims metadata; cross-link with OpenCitations.  [lib.dmu](https://lib.dmu.edu/su/opencitations) |
| **Scite** | Shows how papers are cited (supporting/disputing). | Integrate scite’s “supporting/disputing” signals into evidence evaluation.  [lib.dmu](https://lib.dmu.edu/su/opencitations) |
| **Internet Archive Scholar** | 25M+ research articles preserved openly. | Use for full-text evidence retrieval and archival.  [lib.dmu](https://lib.dmu.edu/su/opencitations) |
| **Dimensions (free version)** | 100M+ publications + grants/trials/patents. | Use for cross-domain evidence (grants, clinical trials).  [lib.dmu](https://lib.dmu.edu/su/opencitations) |
